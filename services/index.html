What can we do to help?

We are able to provide Agile DevSecOps teams which operate on the basis of delivered work rather than fixed requirements. We can also provide individuals to help train teams in various methodologies.

We use Agile to understand our customers vision and will deliver a product early in development to help our customers better define their vision and business needs.

We combine Agile with DevSecOps as it allows us to quickly deliver a working product and then iteratively evolve it. This means our customers will always have a working product which will at first evolve to better meet your business needs and then grow to incorporate new capabilities.

Each project can have differing needs of assurance, we will tailor assurance based on your business requirements and we will incorporate assurance into all Continuous Integration and Continuous Delivery pipelines. Pallas will work with your assurance and security teams to ensure they have the information they need.

We provide development and operations staff so we are able to develop a product and support it in production.

Lastly at Pallas we believe your mission should be the priority and will look to deliver to your needs as soon as possible to maximise the impact of our work.
