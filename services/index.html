What can we do to help?

Pallas will place your vision at the heart of delivery using Agile Scrum to quickly deliver a working product. We will then use your feedback to iteratively tailor the solution to better meet your needs.

Pallas integrates DevSecOps with Agile Scrum to shorten the feedback loop. We will use Continuous Integration (CI) & Continuous Deployment (CD) pipelines to automate build and deployment so we are able to quickly implement your ideas. This allows us to better elaborate your problem space and ensure we are focussed on delivering your vision.

We understand your organisation will have its own rules, processes and procedures, so rather than dictate a single Agile Scrum approach we look to define an Agile approach which integrates with your business and mission needs. We will tailor our assurance based on your business requirements and we will incorporate assurance into all CI and CD pipelines. Pallas will work with your assurance and security teams to ensure they have the information they need.

Pallas will work with you to understand your current platforms and services, we have experience in migrating legacy software on to a mixture of cloud platforms, allowing you to decommission legacy hardware. We can also provide consultancy on refactoring your software to better fit cloud architectures, providing a range of options from minimal adaptions to complete rewrites to take advantage of the latest cloud services.

At Pallas we believe your mission should be the priority and will look to deliver to your needs as soon as possible to maximise the impact of our work.
