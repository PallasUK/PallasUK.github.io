What is Agile?

Agile is an iterative approach to product development which aims to deliver working software quickly. Agile aims to reduce the risk of development by reducing the time between requirements gathering to delivery and acceptence.

The Agile Manifesto outlines a set of principles, including the principle people over process. Scrum and Kanban are development methodologies which encourage teams to follow Agile principles but it is possible for a team to follow a defined Scrum process and fail to achieve Agile principles.

Lastly many organisations have interdependent software development teams. Scaled Agile frameworks (SAFe, Lean-Agile, etc..) have been developed to allow the management of these inter team dependencies.
