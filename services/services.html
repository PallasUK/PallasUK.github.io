What can we do to help?

Pallas has expearence in managing and operating a variety of Agile DevSecOps teams which have delivered small research projects to large support functions.

We use Agile methodology to understand your vision and work with yourselves to agree the priority and delivery of work. We prefer not to work to fixed requirements which can be subject to change request, but instead to agree to deliverable effort and work towards your vision.

We incorporate Minimum Viable Product and DevSecOps to ensure we iteratively deliver a working product as soon as possible. This means for each agreed iteration of work you can be confident you will have a working product.

Each project and business can have differing needs of assurance, we will incorporate assurance into all Continious Integration and Continious Delivery pipelines. Pallas will work with your assurance and security teams to ensure they have the information they need.

We povide development and operations staff so we are able to develop a product and support it in production.

  • Agile Kanban/Scrum development
  • Use of Fail Fast to reduce risk
  • Extraction of metrics to guide business decisions
  • Integrated Developer and Support teams
  • Secure Cloud architectures and development
  • Integration of ITIL with DevSecOps
  • Deployment of Continious Integration and Continious Delivery
  • Consultancy and Coaching Services
