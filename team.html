The Team

Launched in 2022 by PersonA and Steve, our goal is to create a business that places our customer needs and staff first. A place where all engineers can feel welcome and able to share ideas and contribute.

Person A

Founder & DevSecOps Engineer

PersonA has been developing and supporting large scale structured data analysis for years. Bringing knowledge on how to secure systems and process vast amounts of data on small budgets while keeping users happy.

He can usually be found hurtling down mountain sides on his mountain bike, or hiking with his wife and daughter.

Steve

Founder & DevSecOps Engineer

Steve has has worked on research prototypes to technically leading the development and support of large scale unstructured data platforms. Steve is a keen adovocate for process so he can then automate it and is always looking to pull out metrics to understand if things can be improved.

Steve has expearence in converting teams into high performing Agile Scrum teams, acting as a scrum master where needed and is happy to share his understanding of Agile Principles, DevSecOps or the latest technology framework and how they can better enable micro service architectures.

He spends most of his time automating his own home or finding excuses to see steam trains with his son.
